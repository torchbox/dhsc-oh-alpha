Register as an occupational health provider

Occupational health is provided by a range of multidisciplinary professionals who help individuals to remain in, return to, and thrive at work.

They do this by protecting and promoting the health, safety and wellbeing of people in their workplace or in relation to their job.

Use this service to:

  • benchmark your workplace against others offering the same service
  • aid Government understanding of the occupational health workforce and the issues affecting it
  • sign up to our approved supplier list for future contracts

Registering takes around 5 minutes.
