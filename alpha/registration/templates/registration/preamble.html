Register as an occupational health provider

Occupational health providers - from the public and private sector - should use this service to create an Occupational Health Workforce Data Set account.

View eligibility criteria

Use this service to:

  • submit and manage demographic and eployment data about your workforce
  • aid Government understanding of the occupational health workforce and the issues affecting it
  • sign up to our approved supplier list for future contracts
  • sign up to gain approval to access the occupational health innovation support fund

Registering takes around 5 minutes.

Before you start

Some of your workforce must be based in England.

You will need to add some basic details about your organisation and the primary user of this service.

We will need to verify your registration before you are able to submit your workforce data.
